The idea

High-pRofiLe is not a shop.

There's nothing here to add to a cart. You bring the request we bring the supply chain.

Whether it's one item or a full container, we handle the search, the haggling, the quality check and the shipping. You deal with one person, in Pula, instead of a dozen strangers on the other side of the world.

You only ever pay once you've seen the price and said yes.

How it works

From your request to your hands five steps.

No middle-man maze. One conversation, one price, one delivery. Here's the whole journey.

01

Send your request You

Describe the product, paste a link, or send a photo. Tell us the quantity and roughly what you'd like to spend. That's all we need to get moving.

02

We source & vet Us

We hunt down suppliers in China, compare prices, and check the ones worth trusting ratings, samples, history. The bad ones never reach you.

03

You get one price Quote

A single quote in Pula: product, shipping and our fee, itemised. No hidden mark-ups. Accept it or walk away no obligation.

04

We buy & ship Us

Once you accept, we pay the supplier, inspect the goods before they leave China, and ship them to Botswana with a tracking number you can follow.

05

You receive You

Collect your order in Botswana. No customs guesswork, no surprise charges. That's it done.
